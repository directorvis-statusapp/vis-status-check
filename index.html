VIS Volunteer Status Checker

Enter your Registration# to check VOLUNTEER completion status. No login required.

Data source: Hosted CSV (read-only). If your record is incorrect, contact VIS office.

How it works

  1. The page fetches the VIS CSV file from a secure UCP link (read-only).
  2. It looks up the entered Registration# and shows one of three results:
    ✅ Completed 65 hours — Eligible for Transcript
    ⚠️ In progress
    ❌ No record found
